## Quick Tips for Success

{: .note }
> **Start Early** - Most successful grant applications take 3-6 months of preparation
>
> **Read Reviewer Guidelines** - Understanding how your proposal will be evaluated is critical
>
> **Get Feedback** - Have colleagues review your proposal before submission
>
> **Follow Instructions** - Strict adherence to format and page limits is essential
>
> **Build Relationships** - Connect with program officers and previous awardees
