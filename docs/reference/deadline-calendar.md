## üéØ Tips for Deadline Management

### Set Up Alerts

**For Live Grants:**
- Run the scraper monthly: `python src/master_scraper.py`
- Subscribe to [Grants.gov email alerts](https://www.grants.gov/)
- Follow funding agencies on social media

**For Curated Grants:**
- Mark recurring deadlines in your calendar
- Set reminders 6 months before
- Check agency websites quarterly

### Multi-Grant Strategy

Don't put all eggs in one basket! Successful researchers often have:
- **3-5 applications** in preparation at any time
- **Mix of deadlines** across the year
- **Different risk levels** (safe bets + reach grants)

### Resubmission Planning

If rejected:
- ‚úÖ Read reviews immediately
- ‚úÖ Start revisions within 1 month
- ‚úÖ Most agencies allow resubmission
- ‚úÖ Resubmissions have higher success rates
