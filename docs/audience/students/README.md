# Grants for Students / PhD

**31 funding opportunities** for PhD students, undergraduate researchers, and young scholars.

---

## Global Fellowships

*Open to students worldwide*

### [Thiel Fellowship](https://thielfellowship.org/)

**Organization**: Thiel Foundation | **Amount**: $100,000 over 2 years | **Deadline**: Typically summer

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

For young people to pursue ideas outside traditional education

### [O'Shaughnessy Fellowships](https://www.osv.llc/fellowships)

**Organization**: O'Shaughnessy Ventures | **Amount**: $100,000 | **Deadline**: Rolling applications

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Open Tech / OSS / Internet

For ambitious individuals working on breakthrough ideas

### [Google PhD Fellowship](https://research.google/programs-and-events/phd-fellowship/)

**Organization**: Google | **Amount**: $85,000/year for up to 2 years (US/Canada); varies by region | **Deadline**: April-May annually

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI

Supports exceptional PhD students in computer science and related fields. Must be nominated by university

### [Meta Research PhD Fellowship](https://research.facebook.com/fellowship/)

**Organization**: Meta | **Amount**: Tuition + $42,000/year for 2 years | **Deadline**: Typically September

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI

Supports PhD students in AR/VR, AI, ML, computer vision, NLP, and related areas

### [NVIDIA Graduate Fellowship](https://research.nvidia.com/graduate-fellowships)

**Organization**: NVIDIA | **Amount**: Up to $60,000 per award | **Deadline**: September 15 annually

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences

For PhD students in CS, CE, System Architecture, EE. Includes mandatory summer internship at NVIDIA

### [Adobe Research Fellowship](https://research.adobe.com/gem-fellowship/)

**Organization**: Adobe | **Amount**: $10,000 + Creative Cloud subscription | **Deadline**: September (5pm PT)

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI

For full-time PhD students in computer graphics, computer vision, HCI, ML, NLP, AI

### [Apple Scholars in AIML](https://machinelearning.apple.com/updates/apple-scholars-aiml-2024)

**Organization**: Apple | **Amount**: 2-year funding, varies by country | **Deadline**: Nomination-based

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI

For PhD students in AI/ML. Nomination-based program supporting exceptional researchers

### [Outreachy Internships](https://www.outreachy.org/)

**Organization**: Software Freedom Conservancy | **Amount**: $7,000 stipend | **Deadline**: February and August

**Domains**: Open Tech / OSS / Internet, Computer Science & AI

Diversity initiative for open source internships

## United States

### [NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (GRFP)](https://www.nsfgrfp.org/)

**Organization**: National Science Foundation | **Amount**: $37,000/year stipend + $12,000 cost of education | **Deadline**: October (annually)

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy

Fellowship for graduate students in STEM and social sciences

### [NIH F31 Predoctoral Fellowship](https://researchtraining.nih.gov/programs/fellowships/f31)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: $25,000-$27,000 stipend + tuition | **Deadline**: April, August, December

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Individual predoctoral fellowship for biomedical research

### [Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/academic-program/phd-fellowship/)

**Organization**: Microsoft | **Amount**: Full tuition + $42,000/year for 2 years | **Deadline**: October (department chair submissions)

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI

For PhD students in systems, networking, AI, ML, computer vision, and robotics. Must be in third year of PhD

### [IBM PhD Fellowship](https://researchweb.draco.res.ibm.com/university/awards/fellowships.html)

**Organization**: IBM | **Amount**: $60,000 first year, $35,000 second year | **Deadline**: October

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI

For outstanding PhD students in computer science and related technical fields

### [Amazon AI PhD Fellowship](https://www.amazon.science/research-awards)

**Organization**: Amazon | **Amount**: $10M annually distributed across 9 partner universities | **Deadline**: Varies by institution

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI

Supports PhD students at partner institutions (Caltech, CMU, Georgia Tech, MIT, Stanford, UC Berkeley, UCLA, University of Washington, UT Austin)
