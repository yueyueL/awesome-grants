# Grants for Postdocs / Early Career Researchers (ECRs)

**27 funding opportunities** for postdoctoral researchers and early career researchers building independent careers.

---

## Global Fellowships & Programs

*Open to postdocs worldwide*

### [Schmidt Science Fellows](https://schmidtsciencefellows.org/)

**Organization**: Schmidt Sciences | **Amount**: $100,000/year for 2 years | **Deadline**: Typically February

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth

Postdoctoral program for STEM PhDs to pivot to new fields

### [L'Oréal-UNESCO For Women in Science](https://www.forwomeninscience.com/)

**Organization**: L'Oréal Foundation & UNESCO | **Amount**: €15,000 (International Rising Talents) | **Deadline**: Varies by country

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth

Recognition and support for women scientists

### [Fast Grants](https://fastgrants.org/)

**Organization**: Fast Grants | **Amount**: $10,000-$500,000 | **Deadline**: Rolling (rapid decisions)

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Rapid funding for COVID and science projects (varies by availability)

### [Chan Zuckerberg Initiative - Science](https://chanzuckerberg.com/science/)

**Organization**: Chan Zuckerberg Initiative | **Amount**: Variable (typically millions) | **Deadline**: Varies by program

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Computer Science & AI

Various programs supporting biomedical research and AI

## United States

### [NIH K99/R00 Pathway to Independence Award](https://www.niddk.nih.gov/research-funding/process/apply/funding-mechanisms/k-awards/k99-r00)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: Up to $249,000 (K99) + $249,000/year (R00) | **Deadline**: February, June, October

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Transition award from postdoc to independent researcher

### [NIH R01 Research Project Grant](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/r01.htm)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: Variable (typically $250,000-$500,000/year) | **Deadline**: Rolling (multiple cycles)

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Major NIH grant for independent research projects

### [NSF Postdoctoral Research Fellowships](https://www.nsf.gov/funding/education.jsp?fund_type=3)

**Organization**: National Science Foundation | **Amount**: $69,000/year salary + $10,000 research | **Deadline**: Varies by program

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Social Sciences & Policy

Postdoctoral fellowships across NSF disciplines

### [NASA Postdoctoral Program](https://npp.orau.org/)

**Organization**: NASA | **Amount**: $60,000-$80,000/year | **Deadline**: March, July, November

**Domains**: Engineering & Physical Sciences, Environment & Earth, Computer Science & AI

Postdoctoral fellowships at NASA centers

### [NEH Fellowships](https://www.neh.gov/grants/research/fellowships)

**Organization**: National Endowment for the Humanities | **Amount**: $5,000/month for 6-12 months | **Deadline**: April (annually)

**Domains**: Humanities & Arts, Social Sciences & Policy

Support for advanced research in the humanities

## United Kingdom

### [Royal Society University Research Fellowships](https://royalsociety.org/grants/university-research/)

**Organization**: Royal Society (UK) | **Amount**: Up to £1.87M over 8 years (80% FEC) | **Deadline**: Annual (September)

**Domains**: Engineering & Physical Sciences, Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

8-year fellowships for outstanding theoretical scientists 3-8 years post-PhD in natural sciences. Host institution commits to open-ended position. Up to 35 awards annually.

### [Leverhulme Early Career Fellowships](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/early-career-fellowships)

**Organization**: Leverhulme Trust | **Amount**: Year 1: £56K (100%); Years 2-3: £28K+£28K match; plus £6K pa research | **Deadline**: Annual (early February)

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

3-year fellowships for early-career researchers establishing independent research careers. Viva ≤4 years before application. ~145 awards per year.

### [Leverhulme Trust Research Fellowships](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/research-fellowships)

**Organization**: Leverhulme Trust | **Amount**: Salary + £6,000 research expenses | **Deadline**: January (annually)

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

For experienced researchers to undertake significant research

### [UKRI Future Leaders Fellowships](https://www.ukri.org/what-we-do/developing-people-and-skills/future-leaders-fellowships/)

**Organization**: UK Research and Innovation | **Amount**: £1-1.5 million over 4-7 years | **Deadline**: Varies (typically biannual)

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

Support for early career researchers and innovators

### [Wellcome Trust Career Development Awards](https://wellcome.org/grant-funding/schemes/career-development-awards)

**Organization**: Wellcome Trust | **Amount**: £1-2 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Rolling

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

For postdocs and early career researchers in biomedical science

### [British Academy Postdoctoral Fellowships](https://www.thebritishacademy.ac.uk/funding/postdoctoral-fellowships/)

**Organization**: British Academy | **Amount**: 3 years salary + research costs (£350k total) | **Deadline**: October (annually)

**Domains**: Humanities & Arts, Social Sciences & Policy

For early career researchers in humanities and social sciences

## Europe / EU

### [ERC Starting Grant](https://erc.europa.eu/funding/starting-grants)

**Organization**: European Research Council | **Amount**: €1.5 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically October

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

For researchers 2-7 years post-PhD with promising scientific track record

### [Marie Skłodowska-Curie Individual Fellowships](https://marie-sklodowska-curie-actions.ec.europa.eu/actions/postdoctoral-fellowships)

**Organization**: European Commission | **Amount**: €5,000/month + mobility allowance | **Deadline**: September (annually)

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

Postdoctoral fellowships for researchers of any nationality

### [EMBO Long-Term Fellowships](https://www.embo.org/funding/fellowships-grants-and-career-support/postdoctoral-fellowships/)

**Organization**: EMBO | **Amount**: ~€5,000/month + travel/family allowances | **Deadline**: February, August

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Postdoctoral fellowships in life sciences

### [Alexander von Humboldt Fellowship](https://www.humboldt-foundation.de/en/apply/sponsorship-programmes/humboldt-research-fellowship)

**Organization**: Alexander von Humboldt Foundation | **Amount**: €2,670-€3,170/month | **Deadline**: Rolling applications

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

Research stays in Germany for experienced researchers

### [European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL) Interdisciplinary Postdocs](https://www.embl.org/about/info/postdoctoral-programme/)

**Organization**: EMBL | **Amount**: Competitive salary + benefits | **Deadline**: Spring and Fall

**Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Computer Science & AI

Interdisciplinary postdoctoral positions at EMBL

## Canada

### [Canada Postdoctoral Research Award (CPRA)](https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PD-NP/cpra-bprc_eng.asp)

**Organization**: Tri-agency (CIHR, NSERC, SSHRC), Canada | **Amount**: CAD $70,000/year for 2 years | **Deadline**: Annual competition

**Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

Harmonized postdoctoral fellowship program in Canada for exceptional applicants nationally and internationally. This program replaces the former Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships program.
