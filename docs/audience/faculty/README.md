# Grants for Faculty / PI

**30 funding opportunities** for research leaders and principal investigators establishing independent research programs.

---

## Research Project Grants

*Large collaborative grants supporting specific research programs, teams, and infrastructure*

### [Howard Hughes Medical Institute (HHMI) Investigators](https://www.hhmi.org/programs/investigators)

**Organization**: HHMI | **Amount**: ~$9-11 million over 7 years | **Deadline**: Nomination-based (every ~3 years)

**Region**: US | **Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Major support for outstanding biomedical researchers

### [ERC Advanced Grant](https://erc.europa.eu/funding/advanced-grants)

**Organization**: European Research Council | **Amount**: €2.5 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically May

**Region**: EU / Horizon | **Domains**: All fields

For established research leaders with significant track record

### [ERC Consolidator Grant](https://erc.europa.eu/funding/consolidator-grants)

**Organization**: European Research Council | **Amount**: €2 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically January

**Region**: EU / Horizon | **Domains**: All fields

For researchers 7-12 years post-PhD to consolidate their teams

### [Horizon Europe Research and Innovation Actions](https://research-and-innovation.ec.europa.eu/funding/funding-opportunities/funding-programmes-and-open-calls/horizon-europe_en)

**Organization**: European Commission | **Amount**: Variable (€3-10 million typical) | **Deadline**: Multiple calls throughout year

**Region**: EU / Horizon | **Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy

Large collaborative research projects

### [ERC Starting Grant](https://erc.europa.eu/funding/starting-grants)

**Organization**: European Research Council | **Amount**: €1.5 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically October

**Region**: EU / Horizon | **Domains**: All fields

For researchers 2-7 years post-PhD with promising scientific track record

### [A*STAR Investigatorship (Singapore)](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/aei)

**Organization**: Agency for Science, Technology and Research | **Amount**: Up to S$3 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically twice a year

**Region**: Asia-Pacific | **Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

For outstanding scientists to establish independent research programs

### [DOE Early Career Research Program](https://science.osti.gov/early-career)

**Organization**: Department of Energy | **Amount**: $750,000 over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically May (annually)

**Region**: US | **Domains**: Engineering & Physical Sciences, Computer Science & AI, Environment & Earth

Support for early-career university faculty in energy-related research

### [DARPA Young Faculty Award (YFA)](https://www.darpa.mil/work-with-us/communities/academia/young-faculty-award)

**Organization**: DARPA | **Amount**: $500,000 over 2 years (Phase I) | **Deadline**: Typically August-September

**Region**: US | **Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences

Support for innovative research by junior faculty

### [NSF CAREER Award](https://www.nsf.gov/funding/pgm_summ.jsp?pims_id=503214)

**Organization**: National Science Foundation | **Amount**: $400,000 - $500,000 over 5 years | **Deadline**: July (annually)

**Region**: US | **Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy

Early-career faculty development award

### [NIH R01 Research Project Grant](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/r01.htm)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: Variable (typically $250,000-$500,000/year) | **Deadline**: Rolling (multiple cycles)

**Region**: US | **Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Major NIH grant for independent research projects

### [EPSRC New Investigator Awards](https://www.ukri.org/councils/epsrc/guidance-for-applicants/types-of-funding-we-offer/)

**Organization**: EPSRC | **Amount**: Up to £250,000 | **Deadline**: Rolling

**Region**: UK | **Domains**: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences

For early career principal investigators in engineering and physical sciences

### [Kavli Foundation Programs](https://www.kavlifoundation.org/)

**Organization**: The Kavli Foundation | **Amount**: Variable; $100 million+ commitments for institutes | **Deadline**: By invitation/institutional partnership

**Region**: Global | **Domains**: Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

20 Kavli Institutes worldwide in astrophysics, nanoscience, neuroscience, and theoretical physics

### [Simons Foundation Investigator Awards](https://www.simonsfoundation.org/funding-opportunities/)

**Organization**: Simons Foundation | **Amount**: $100,000-$200,000/year for 5 years | **Deadline**: Varies by program

**Region**: US, Global | **Domains**: Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Computer Science & AI, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Support for outstanding theoretical scientists

### [Chan Zuckerberg Initiative - Science](https://chanzuckerberg.com/science/)

**Organization**: Chan Zuckerberg Initiative | **Amount**: Variable (typically millions) | **Deadline**: Varies by program

**Region**: Global | **Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Computer Science & AI

Various programs supporting biomedical research and AI

### [Fast Grants](https://fastgrants.org/)

**Organization**: Fast Grants | **Amount**: $10,000-$500,000 | **Deadline**: Rolling (rapid decisions)

**Region**: Global | **Domains**: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

Rapid funding for COVID and science projects (varies by availability)
