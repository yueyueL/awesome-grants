# United States Grants

**15 funding opportunities for US-based researchers**

Federal funding agencies and major US foundations supporting research and innovation.

---

## Faculty / PI

### [NSF CAREER Award](https://www.nsf.gov/funding/pgm_summ.jsp?pims_id=503214)

**Organization**: National Science Foundation | **Amount**: $400,000 - $500,000 over 5 years | **Deadline**: July (annually)

Early-career faculty development award

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy

### [NIH R01 Research Project Grant](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/r01.htm)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: Variable (typically $250,000-$500,000/year) | **Deadline**: Rolling (multiple cycles)

Major NIH grant for independent research projects

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [DOE Early Career Research Program](https://science.osti.gov/early-career)

**Organization**: Department of Energy | **Amount**: $750,000 over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically May (annually)

Support for early-career university faculty in energy-related research

*Domains*: Engineering & Physical Sciences, Computer Science & AI, Environment & Earth

### [DARPA Young Faculty Award (YFA)](https://www.darpa.mil/work-with-us/communities/academia/young-faculty-award)

**Organization**: DARPA | **Amount**: $500,000 over 2 years (Phase I) | **Deadline**: Typically August-September

Support for innovative research by junior faculty

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences

### [NEH Fellowships](https://www.neh.gov/grants/research/fellowships)

**Organization**: National Endowment for the Humanities | **Amount**: $5,000/month for 6-12 months | **Deadline**: April (annually)

Support for advanced research in the humanities

*Domains*: Humanities & Arts, Social Sciences & Policy

### [Sloan Research Fellowships](https://sloan.org/fellowships)

**Organization**: Alfred P. Sloan Foundation | **Amount**: $75,000 over 2 years | **Deadline**: September (annually)

For early-career scholars of exceptional promise

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [Packard Fellowships for Science and Engineering](https://www.packard.org/what-we-fund/science/packard-fellowships-for-science-and-engineering/)

**Organization**: David and Lucile Packard Foundation | **Amount**: $875,000 over 5 years | **Deadline**: Nomination-based

For innovative early-career scientists and engineers

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth

### [Pew Scholars Program in Biomedical Sciences](https://www.pewtrusts.org/en/projects/pew-biomedical-scholars)

**Organization**: Pew Charitable Trusts | **Amount**: $300,000 over 4 years | **Deadline**: Nomination-based (October)

For early-career biomedical researchers

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [Howard Hughes Medical Institute (HHMI) Investigators](https://www.hhmi.org/programs/investigators)

**Organization**: HHMI | **Amount**: ~$9-11 million over 7 years | **Deadline**: Nomination-based (every ~3 years)

Major support for outstanding biomedical researchers

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [Simons Foundation Investigator Awards](https://www.simonsfoundation.org/funding-opportunities/)

**Organization**: Simons Foundation | **Amount**: $100,000-$200,000/year for 5 years | **Deadline**: Varies by program

Support for outstanding theoretical scientists

*Domains*: Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Computer Science & AI, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

## Postdocs / ECRs

### [NIH R01 Research Project Grant](https://grants.nih.gov/grants/funding/r01.htm)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: Variable (typically $250,000-$500,000/year) | **Deadline**: Rolling (multiple cycles)

Major NIH grant for independent research projects

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [NIH K99/R00 Pathway to Independence Award](https://www.niddk.nih.gov/research-funding/process/apply/funding-mechanisms/k-awards/k99-r00)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: Up to $249,000 (K99) + $249,000/year (R00) | **Deadline**: February, June, October

Transition award from postdoc to independent researcher

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [NASA Postdoctoral Program](https://npp.orau.org/)

**Organization**: NASA | **Amount**: $60,000-$80,000/year | **Deadline**: March, July, November

Postdoctoral fellowships at NASA centers

*Domains*: Engineering & Physical Sciences, Environment & Earth, Computer Science & AI

### [NSF Postdoctoral Research Fellowships](https://www.nsf.gov/funding/education.jsp?fund_type=3)

**Organization**: National Science Foundation | **Amount**: $69,000/year salary + $10,000 research | **Deadline**: Varies by program

Postdoctoral fellowships across NSF disciplines

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Social Sciences & Policy

### [NEH Fellowships](https://www.neh.gov/grants/research/fellowships)

**Organization**: National Endowment for the Humanities | **Amount**: $5,000/month for 6-12 months | **Deadline**: April (annually)

Support for advanced research in the humanities

*Domains*: Humanities & Arts, Social Sciences & Policy

## Students / PhD

### [NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (GRFP)](https://www.nsfgrfp.org/)

**Organization**: National Science Foundation | **Amount**: $37,000/year stipend + $12,000 cost of education | **Deadline**: October (annually)

Fellowship for graduate students in STEM and social sciences

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy

### [NIH F31 Predoctoral Fellowship](https://researchtraining.nih.gov/programs/fellowships/f31)

**Organization**: National Institutes of Health | **Amount**: $25,000-$27,000 stipend + tuition | **Deadline**: April, August, December

Individual predoctoral fellowship for biomedical research

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine
