# United Kingdom Grants

**7 funding opportunities for UK-based researchers**

UKRI, Wellcome Trust, Royal Society, and other major UK funding bodies.

---

## Faculty / PI

### [UKRI Future Leaders Fellowships](https://www.ukri.org/what-we-do/developing-people-and-skills/future-leaders-fellowships/)

**Organization**: UK Research and Innovation | **Amount**: £1-1.5 million over 4-7 years | **Deadline**: Varies (typically biannual)

Support for early career researchers and innovators

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

### [Wellcome Trust Senior Research Fellowships](https://wellcome.org/grant-funding/schemes/senior-research-fellowships)

**Organization**: Wellcome Trust | **Amount**: Up to £3 million over 5-8 years | **Deadline**: Rolling

For exceptional researchers with independent track record

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [Leverhulme Trust Research Fellowships](https://www.leverhulme.ac.uk/research-fellowships)

**Organization**: Leverhulme Trust | **Amount**: Salary + £6,000 research expenses | **Deadline**: January (annually)

For experienced researchers to undertake significant research

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

### [EPSRC New Investigator Awards](https://www.ukri.org/councils/epsrc/guidance-for-applicants/types-of-funding-we-offer/)

**Organization**: EPSRC | **Amount**: Up to £250,000 | **Deadline**: Rolling

For early career principal investigators in engineering and physical sciences

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences
