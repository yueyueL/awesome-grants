# Asia-Pacific Grants

**6 funding opportunities for Asia-Pacific researchers**

Grants from Singapore, Australia, Japan, and other regional programs.

---

## Faculty / PI

### [NRF Fellowship (Singapore)](https://www.nrf.gov.sg/grants/nrff/)

**Organization**: National Research Foundation Singapore | **Amount**: Up to S$3.5 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically June

For outstanding early-career researchers to start independent careers in Singapore

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [ARC Future Fellowship](https://www.arc.gov.au/funding-research/funding-schemes)

**Organization**: Australian Research Council | **Amount**: A$1.1 million over 4 years | **Deadline**: Varies

For mid-career researchers to undertake research in Australia

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Data & Math, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine, Environment & Earth, Social Sciences & Policy, Humanities & Arts

### [A*STAR Investigatorship (Singapore)](https://www.a-star.edu.sg/aei)

**Organization**: Agency for Science, Technology and Research | **Amount**: Up to S$3 million over 5 years | **Deadline**: Typically twice a year

For outstanding scientists to establish independent research programs

*Domains*: Computer Science & AI, Engineering & Physical Sciences, Life Sciences & Biomedicine

### [NHMRC Investigator Grants (Australia)](https://www.nhmrc.gov.au/funding/find-funding/investigator-grants)

**Organization**: National Health and Medical Research Council | **Amount**: Variable (5 years) | **Deadline**: March (annually)

Support for health and medical researchers at different career stages

*Domains*: Life Sciences & Biomedicine
