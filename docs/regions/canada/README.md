# Canada Grants

Funding opportunities for Canadian researchers from federal agencies and provincial programs.

---

## Federal Funding

### [Vanier Canada Graduate Scholarships](https://vanier.gc.ca/en/home-accueil.html)

**Organization**: NSERC, CIHR, SSHRC | **Amount**: CAD $50,000/year for 3 years | **Audience**: Students / PhD

Doctoral scholarships for exceptional students

**Website**: https://vanier.gc.ca/en/home-accueil.html

### [Canada Postdoctoral Research Award (CPRA)](https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PD-NP/cpra-bprc_eng.asp)

**Organization**: NSERC | **Amount**: CAD $70,000/year for 2 years | **Audience**: Postdocs / ECRs

Replaces the former Banting Postdoctoral Fellowships. Support for top-tier postdoctoral researchers.

**Website**: https://www.nserc-crsng.gc.ca/Students-Etudiants/PD-NP/cpra-bprc_eng.asp

### NSERC Discovery Grants

**Organization**: Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada | **Amount**: Variable | **Audience**: Faculty / PI

Support for fundamental research in natural sciences and engineering

### CIHR Project Grants

**Organization**: Canadian Institutes of Health Research | **Amount**: Variable | **Audience**: Faculty / PI

Health research funding
