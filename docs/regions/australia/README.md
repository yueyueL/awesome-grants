# Australia Grants

Funding opportunities for Australian researchers from ARC, NHMRC, and other agencies.

---

## Australian Research Council (ARC)

### [ARC Discovery Projects](https://www.arc.gov.au/funding-research/discovery-linkage/discovery-program/discovery-projects)

**Organization**: Australian Research Council | **Amount**: $30,000-$500,000/year for up to 5 years | **Audience**: Faculty / PI

Support for fundamental research across all disciplines

**Website**: https://www.arc.gov.au/funding-research/discovery-linkage/discovery-program/discovery-projects

### [ARC DECRA](https://www.arc.gov.au/funding-research/funding-schemes/discovery-program/discovery-early-career-researcher-award-decra)

**Organization**: Australian Research Council | **Amount**: A$465,000 over 3 years | **Audience**: Postdocs / ECRs

Discovery Early Career Researcher Award

**Website**: https://www.arc.gov.au/funding-research/funding-schemes/discovery-program/discovery-early-career-researcher-award-decra

### [ARC Future Fellowship](https://www.arc.gov.au/funding-research/funding-schemes)

**Organization**: Australian Research Council | **Amount**: A$1.1 million over 4 years | **Audience**: Faculty / PI

For mid-career researchers to undertake research in Australia

**Website**: https://www.arc.gov.au/funding-research/funding-schemes

## National Health and Medical Research Council (NHMRC)

### [NHMRC Investigator Grants](https://www.nhmrc.gov.au/funding/find-funding/investigator-grants)

**Organization**: National Health and Medical Research Council | **Amount**: Variable (5 years) | **Audience**: Faculty / PI, Postdocs / ECRs

Support for health and medical researchers at different career stages

**Website**: https://www.nhmrc.gov.au/funding/find-funding/investigator-grants
