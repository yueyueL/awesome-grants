## Quick Comparison

### Fellowships
Individual researcher support with salary + research budget. Best for:
- Graduate students (NSF GRFP, NIH F31)
- Postdocs (Marie Curie, EMBO, K99/R00)
- Faculty (Royal Society, UKRI Future Leaders)

### Industry PhD Fellowships
Company-sponsored PhD support with funding, mentorship, and career opportunities. Best for:
- Tech/AI (Intel, Two Sigma, Google, Meta, NVIDIA, ByteDance)
- Finance (Jane Street, Citadel, Jump Trading, BlackRock)
- Semiconductors (SRC, Broadcom, AMD, TI)
- Pharma/Biotech (Biogen, GSK, AstraZeneca, J&J)
- Defense/Aerospace (NDSEG, SMART, Northrop Grumman, BMW)
- Energy (Schlumberger, Chevron, ExxonMobil)

### Research Project Grants
Team-based funding for specific projects. Best for:
- Early career faculty (NSF CAREER, ERC Starting)
- Established PIs (NIH R01, ARC Discovery)
- Large collaborations (Horizon Europe)

### OSS Grants
Open source software development funding. Best for:
- Infrastructure projects (Sovereign Tech Fund)
- Development (NLnet, Mozilla, Prototype Fund)
- Community building (GitHub Sponsors, NumFOCUS)

### Microgrants
Quick, small funding for experiments and pilots. Best for:
- Testing ideas (Emergent Ventures, AI Grant)
- Side projects (Awesome Foundation)
- Indie builders (Unitary Fund)
