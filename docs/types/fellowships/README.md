# Fellowships

**29 funding opportunities**

*Individual awards providing salary and research support for researchers at various career stages.*

---

## Overview

Fellowships are prestigious awards that provide comprehensive support for individual researchers, including salary, research expenses, and often career development opportunities. They are available at all career stages from PhD students to senior faculty.

### Key Features
- **Duration**: Typically 2-8 years
- **Amount**: Ranges from $25,000/year (student fellowships) to £3 million total (senior fellowships)
- **Support**: Usually covers salary + research costs + sometimes travel/family allowances
- **Mobility**: Many fellowships support international mobility and career transitions

## Application Tips

### Eligibility
- Check citizenship/residency requirements carefully
- Note career stage restrictions (years since PhD, current position)
- Some fellowships require institutional nomination

### Strong Applications Include
- Clear, compelling research proposal
- Evidence of promise/track record appropriate to career stage
- Letters of support from established researchers
- Well-defined career development plan
- Alignment with funder's mission and priorities

### Timeline
- Start preparing 3-6 months before deadline
- Many fellowships are annual competitions
- Some offer rolling submissions
- Nomination-based awards require early departmental coordination
