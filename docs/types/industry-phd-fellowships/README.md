# Industry PhD Fellowships

**60+ industry-sponsored PhD fellowships** spanning technology, finance, semiconductors, pharmaceuticals, defense, energy, and materials science.

*Industry PhD fellowships offer funding from $10,000 one-time awards to $250,000 over five years, with geographic reach spanning the US, Europe, India, and developing economies.*

---

## Technology & Computing Companies

### [Intel PhD Fellowship Program](https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/research/intel-research.html)

**Organization**: Intel | **Amount**: Full tuition, fees, and stipend for one academic year + equipment and travel grants | **Deadline**: Nomination-based

**Eligibility**: U.S. only; unrestricted work authorization; within 18 months of graduation; passed qualifying exams

**Fields**: Hardware systems, semiconductor technology, software technology, social research/ethnography

Nomination-based from select U.S. universities; cannot apply directly. Intel mentor assigned; approximately 26 fellowships awarded annually.

### [Two Sigma PhD Fellowship](https://www.twosigma.com/graduate-students/phd-fellowships/)

**Organization**: Two Sigma | **Amount**: $75,000/year for 2 years + $10,000 one-time award = $160,000 total | **Deadline**: September 20 (opens August 15)

**Eligibility**: U.S. universities; must be in at least 3rd year with 2+ years remaining; cannot hold other industry fellowships

**Fields**: Applied mathematics, computer science, statistics, physics, finance/econometrics

Changed from nomination-based to direct student application in 2025; 500-word research proposal required.

### [Cisco CNI PhD Fellowships (India)](https://www.cisco.com/)

**Organization**: Cisco | **Amount**: ₹43,750/month stipend (1.25× IISc rate) + ₹2,00,000 travel grant + ₹40,000/year contingency | **Deadline**: Variable (renewable annually)

**Eligibility**: India only; must attend Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bengaluru; passed comprehensive exam

**Fields**: Networked intelligence, smart cities, IoT, data center networks, 5G/6G, distributed AI, autonomous systems

EECS Division or RBCCPS at IISc; cannot hold other named external fellowships (TCS, Qualcomm, Visvesvaraya, Google).

### [Oracle for Research Fellows Program](https://www.oracle.com/research/fellows-program/)

**Organization**: Oracle | **Amount**: Significant research funding (varies) + full Oracle Cloud Infrastructure credits | **Deadline**: Rolling

**Eligibility**: U.S., Canada, UK, or Australia; faculty-focused (not direct to PhD students)

**Fields**: Computational research across disciplines utilizing Oracle Cloud Infrastructure

Includes PR/marketing support, webinar platform, conference attendance support, solutions architect support.

### [Snap Research Fellowship](https://research.snap.com/)

**Organization**: Snap Inc. | **Amount**: $10,000 award + conference travel/publishing funding + optional paid internship | **Deadline**: Last confirmed 2022

**Eligibility**: Global; PhD or MS students 18+ at accredited institutions

**Fields**: Computer graphics, computer vision, ML, data mining, computational imaging, HCI, AI, audio/speech, NLP, security

Fellowship includes option to intern at Snap Research (Summer or Fall). Current status unclear.

### [NetApp Faculty Fellowship](https://www.netapp.com/)

**Organization**: NetApp | **Amount**: Varies by proposal | **Deadline**: Rolling

**Eligibility**: U.S. universities primarily; faculty-focused (not direct to PhD students)

**Fields**: Storage and data management, cloud storage, distributed systems, file systems, data center architecture

Faculty member; research proposal demonstrating significant contributions to storage/data management.

### [ByteDance Scholarship Program](https://scholarship.bytedance.com/)

**Organization**: ByteDance | **Amount**: RMB 100,000 (~$13,800 USD) + mentorship + offer acceleration | **Deadline**: Typically May

**Eligibility**: Greater China (Mainland, Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan) and Singapore universities

**Fields**: Computer Science, Fundamental Sciences, AI (LLMs, ML Systems, Multimodality, AI for Science, Embodied Intelligence)

Direct application with resume, 1-2 recommendation letters, proof of research ability (papers, patents, awards).

## Semiconductor Companies

### [Texas Instruments Analog Excellence Graduate Fellowship](https://txace.utdallas.edu/)

**Organization**: Texas Instruments | **Amount**: $25,000/year fellowship + RA stipend + tuition waiver = ~$70,000/year total | **Deadline**: Fall semester

**Duration**: 4 years (contingent on good standing)

**Eligibility**: University of Texas at Dallas only

**Fields**: Analog circuit design and engineering

PhD program at Erik Jonsson School; RA appointment sponsored by UTD faculty.

### [AMD Graduate Research Fellowship](https://www.ece.ucf.edu/industry-sponsored-scholarships/amd-graduate-research-fellowship/)

**Organization**: AMD | **Amount**: Full tuition + sizable stipend for 1 year | **Deadline**: Variable

**Eligibility**: University of Central Florida ECE department only

**Fields**: Generative AI for computer architecture, HPC, VLSI circuits, acceleration of generative/AI algorithms

Identified doctoral advisor at UCF; research proposal and advisor support letter; opportunity for AMD internship interviews.

### [Broadcom Foundation Fellowship Program](https://engineering.uci.edu/broadcom-foundation)

**Organization**: Broadcom Foundation | **Amount**: $25,000 annually (~$47,500 for non-residents including out-of-state tuition) | **Deadline**: Annual

**Duration**: 1 year, renewable for 1 additional year based on merit

**Eligibility**: UC Irvine and UCLA only (10 fellowships at each school annually)

**Fields**: Integrated circuits, embedded systems, sensors/actuators/controllers, electromagnetic components, 3-D packaging, semiconductors

PhD students who passed preliminary exams in EE/CS; 5-page research proposal. Participation in joint UCI/UCLA annual spring symposium.

### [Semiconductor Research Corporation (SRC) Graduate Fellowship Program](https://www.src.org/student-center/fellowship/)

**Organization**: Semiconductor Research Corporation | **Amount**: Full tuition + competitive stipend + $2,000 annual gift to faculty advisor | **Deadline**: April 30

**Duration**: Up to 3 years (36 months)

**Eligibility**: U.S. institutions; U.S. citizens or permanent residents; preference for students at universities with SRC research contracts

**Fields**: Microelectronics (analog/mixed-signal circuit design, AI hardware, packaging, test and reliability)

~35 fellowships per year.

**Duration**: 6-12 months

**Eligibility**: Varies by program (US, UK, other locations)

**Fields**: Equity research (science/medical/healthcare), macro research (economics/finance)

PhD candidates or recent graduates with non-finance backgrounds; career transition program. More internship/transition program than traditional fellowship.

**Fields**: 24 STEM disciplines including aeronautical/aerospace/biomedical/chemical/computer/electrical/mechanical/software engineering, data science, computer science

**CRITICAL**: One-for-one service commitment as civilian DoD employee post-graduation.

## Cross-Industry & Consortium Programs

### [GEM Fellowship Program](https://www.gemfellowship.org/gem-fellowship-program/)

Intern for 2 summers with sponsoring GEM Employer; apply to minimum 3 GEM Member Universities. Dozens of top engineering and science firms as industry partners.

### [Hertz Foundation Fellowship](https://hertzfoundation.org/)

**Eligibility**: U.S. citizens or permanent residents; must attend participating university (50+ agreements)

15-20 per year from ~600-800 applications (1.5% acceptance rate). Industry Partners: Gates Foundation, Analog Devices, Breakthrough Energy Discovery, Lawrence Livermore National Lab. Mentoring, symposia, lifelong network access.
